# Why QuantDesk

A short take on **values**; the full product story is in [What is QuantDesk?](./what-is-quantdesk).

## Principles

- **Terminal-grade first** — depth and speed for people who actually trade, not only for onboarding funnels.  
- **Honest scope** — we say when something is prototype, roadmap, or production.  
- **Non-custodial mindset** — keys stay with users; integration paths are explicit.  
- **Builders included** — APIs and docs for developers, not an afterthought.  
- **No fake “complete”** — crypto finance moves fast; we document what is true today and what is next.

## In one line

QuantDesk is where **serious perp execution**, **clear data and context**, and **integrator-grade APIs** meet in one product story — without dumbing down the market or the stack.
