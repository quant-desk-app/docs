# Strategy collaboration

**Status:** Mix of **prototype** and **roadmap** unless the product explicitly ships a named flow — verify in-app before relying on any workflow described here.

## What we’re aiming for

Spaces where traders and teams can:

- Align on **thesis and invalidation** without noise  
- Share **watchlists and context** tied to markets  
- Coordinate **review cycles** on setups (not guaranteed execution advice)

## What this is not

- Not a substitute for compliance or investment advice  
- Not a guarantee of strategy profitability  
- Not an endorsement of any third-party signal seller  

## Practical posture for readers

Use QuantDesk collaboration features **when labeled shipped** in the app; otherwise assume experimentation.
