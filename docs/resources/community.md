## Community principles

- **Honesty first** — we label shipped vs prototype vs roadmap, and expect the same candor in return.
- **Non-custodial mindset** — your keys, your funds; we build tools, not custody.
- **No financial advice** — community discussion is not investment guidance. Trade your own risk.
